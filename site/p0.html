The Main Parts of the Treaty of Versailles Included:

  • 1. Lost land in the creation of Poland, Czechoslovakia, and the loss of all oversea colonies
  • 2. Demilitarizing of the Rhineland
  • 3. Limiting the army to 100,000 people and not having a naval or air force
  • 4. Article 231: War Guilt Clause
  • 5. No Anschluss (unification of Germany and Austria)
  • 6. Reparations (needing to pay money)
